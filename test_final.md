## Adding and Subtracting Integers

Example 1 Evaluate 4 + (-12).

$$
4+(-12)=-8
$$

Example 2 Evaluate $-7-(-16)$.

$$
\begin{aligned}
-7-(-16) & =-7+16 & & \text { Add the opposite of }-16 . \\
& =9 & & \text { Add. }
\end{aligned}
$$
