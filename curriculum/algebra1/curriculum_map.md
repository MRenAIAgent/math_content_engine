# Algebra 1 Curriculum Map

A unit-by-unit breakdown of Algebra 1 topics with suggested pacing for a full school year (~180 days).

---

## Unit 1: Foundations of Algebra (15 days)

### Topics
1. Variables and Expressions
2. Order of Operations (PEMDAS)
3. Real Numbers and the Number Line
4. Properties of Real Numbers (Commutative, Associative, Distributive)
5. Adding and Subtracting Real Numbers
6. Multiplying and Dividing Real Numbers
7. The Distributive Property
8. Introduction to Equations
9. Patterns and Graphs

### Key Vocabulary
- Variable, constant, coefficient, term, expression
- Integers, rational numbers, irrational numbers
- Absolute value

### Standards Covered
- N-Q.A.1, N-Q.A.2, A-SSE.A.1

---

## Unit 2: Solving Equations (20 days)

### Topics
1. One-Step Equations (Addition/Subtraction)
2. One-Step Equations (Multiplication/Division)
3. Two-Step Equations
4. Multi-Step Equations
5. Equations with Variables on Both Sides
6. Literal Equations and Formulas
7. Ratios and Rates
8. Solving Proportions
9. Similar Figures and Scale Drawings
10. Percent Problems

### Key Vocabulary
- Equation, solution, inverse operations
- Ratio, rate, proportion, scale factor
- Percent, markup, discount

### Standards Covered
- A-CED.A.1, A-CED.A.4, A-REI.A.1, A-REI.B.3

---

## Unit 3: Solving Inequalities (15 days)

### Topics
1. Graphing Inequalities on a Number Line
2. Solving Inequalities Using Addition/Subtraction
3. Solving Inequalities Using Multiplication/Division
4. Multi-Step Inequalities
5. Compound Inequalities (AND / OR)
6. Absolute Value Equations
7. Absolute Value Inequalities

### Key Vocabulary
- Inequality, solution set
- Compound inequality, intersection, union
- Absolute value

### Standards Covered
- A-CED.A.1, A-CED.A.3, A-REI.B.3

---

## Unit 4: Introduction to Functions (18 days)

### Topics
1. Graphing Relationships
2. Relations and Functions
3. Function Notation
4. Evaluating Functions
5. Domain and Range
6. Identifying Linear vs. Nonlinear Functions
7. Graphing Functions from Tables
8. Writing Function Rules
9. Arithmetic Sequences
10. Geometric Sequences (Introduction)

### Key Vocabulary
- Relation, function, input, output
- Domain, range, function notation f(x)
- Sequence, term, common difference

### Standards Covered
- F-IF.A.1, F-IF.A.2, F-IF.A.3, F-IF.B.5, F-BF.A.1, F-BF.A.2

---

## Unit 5: Linear Functions (22 days)

### Topics
1. Rate of Change
2. Slope of a Line
3. Slope Formula
4. Direct Variation
5. Slope-Intercept Form (y = mx + b)
6. Graphing Linear Equations
7. Writing Linear Equations from Graphs
8. Point-Slope Form
9. Standard Form (Ax + By = C)
10. Parallel and Perpendicular Lines
11. Scatter Plots and Trend Lines
12. Linear Regression

### Key Vocabulary
- Slope, rise, run, y-intercept, x-intercept
- Direct variation, constant of variation
- Line of best fit, correlation

### Standards Covered
- F-IF.B.4, F-IF.B.6, F-IF.C.7a, F-LE.A.1, F-LE.A.2, S-ID.B.6, S-ID.C.7, S-ID.C.8

---

## Unit 6: Systems of Equations and Inequalities (18 days)

### Topics
1. Graphing Systems of Equations
2. Types of Solutions (One, None, Infinitely Many)
3. Solving Systems by Substitution
4. Solving Systems by Elimination (Addition)
5. Solving Systems by Elimination (Multiplication)
6. Applications of Systems
7. Graphing Linear Inequalities in Two Variables
8. Systems of Linear Inequalities

### Key Vocabulary
- System of equations, solution of a system
- Consistent, inconsistent, dependent, independent
- Half-plane, boundary line

### Standards Covered
- A-CED.A.2, A-CED.A.3, A-REI.C.5, A-REI.C.6, A-REI.D.11, A-REI.D.12

---

## Unit 7: Exponents and Exponential Functions (15 days)

### Topics
1. Zero and Negative Exponents
2. Product of Powers Property
3. Quotient of Powers Property
4. Power of a Power Property
5. Power of a Product/Quotient
6. Simplifying Expressions with Exponents
7. Scientific Notation
8. Exponential Functions
9. Exponential Growth
10. Exponential Decay

### Key Vocabulary
- Exponent, base, power
- Scientific notation
- Growth factor, decay factor, asymptote

### Standards Covered
- A-SSE.B.3c, F-IF.C.7e, F-IF.C.8b, F-LE.A.1, F-LE.A.2, F-LE.A.3, F-LE.B.5

---

## Unit 8: Polynomials and Factoring (20 days)

### Topics
1. Adding and Subtracting Polynomials
2. Multiplying a Polynomial by a Monomial
3. Multiplying Binomials (FOIL)
4. Multiplying Polynomials
5. Special Products (Perfect Squares, Difference of Squares)
6. Factoring Using the GCF
7. Factoring x² + bx + c
8. Factoring ax² + bx + c
9. Factoring Special Cases
10. Factoring Completely

### Key Vocabulary
- Polynomial, monomial, binomial, trinomial
- Degree, leading coefficient
- Factor, GCF, difference of squares, perfect square trinomial

### Standards Covered
- A-SSE.A.2, A-SSE.B.3a, A-APR.A.1, A-APR.B.3

---

## Unit 9: Quadratic Functions and Equations (22 days)

### Topics
1. Graphing Quadratic Functions (Parabolas)
2. Properties of Quadratics (Vertex, Axis of Symmetry)
3. Quadratic Functions in Vertex Form
4. Quadratic Functions in Standard Form
5. Solving Quadratics by Graphing
6. Solving Quadratics by Factoring
7. Solving Quadratics Using Square Roots
8. Completing the Square
9. The Quadratic Formula
10. The Discriminant
11. Comparing Linear, Quadratic, and Exponential Models
12. Systems with Quadratic Equations

### Key Vocabulary
- Parabola, vertex, axis of symmetry
- Maximum, minimum, roots, zeros, solutions
- Discriminant, quadratic formula

### Standards Covered
- A-SSE.B.3a, A-SSE.B.3b, A-REI.B.4, A-REI.C.7, F-IF.B.4, F-IF.C.7a, F-IF.C.8a

---

### Topics

### Key Vocabulary
- Mean, median, mode, range
- Quartile, interquartile range, outlier
