# Algebra 1 Animation Topics

Topics particularly well-suited for visual/animated explanations, organized by unit.

---

## High-Impact Animation Topics

These topics benefit most from visual representation and step-by-step animation.

### Solving Equations (Visual Balance Method)
- **One-step equations** - Show balance scale analogy
- **Two-step equations** - Animate isolating the variable
- **Equations with variables on both sides** - Visual grouping
- **Solving proportions** - Cross multiplication animation

### Graphing and Coordinate Plane
- **Plotting points on coordinate plane** - Interactive grid
- **Slope as rise over run** - Animated slope triangles
- **Graphing linear equations** - Point-by-point plotting
- **Slope-intercept form (y = mx + b)** - Show how m and b affect the line
- **Parallel and perpendicular lines** - Compare slopes visually

### Systems of Equations
- **Graphing systems** - Two lines intersecting (or parallel/same)
- **Substitution method** - Step-by-step replacement
- **Elimination method** - Adding/subtracting equations visually
- **Types of solutions** - One solution, no solution, infinite solutions

### Quadratic Functions
- **Graphing parabolas** - Vertex, axis of symmetry, direction
- **Vertex form transformations** - How a, h, k affect the graph
- **Solving by graphing** - Finding x-intercepts (zeros)
- **Quadratic formula** - Derive from completing the square
- **Discriminant** - Visualize 2 roots, 1 root, or no real roots

### Exponential Functions
- **Exponential growth** - Doubling, population growth
- **Exponential decay** - Half-life, depreciation
- **Comparing linear vs exponential** - Side-by-side growth
