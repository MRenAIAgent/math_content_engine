# Illustrative Mathematics (IM) Curriculum Resources

*Downloaded: January 2026*

## About Illustrative Mathematics

Illustrative Mathematics (IM) is the most widely adopted math curriculum in US public schools. It is:
- **FREE** under Creative Commons license (CC BY 4.0 for 6-8, CC BY-NC 4.0 for 9-12)
- **Problem-based** learning approach
- **EdReports highest rated** curriculum
- **Mandated in NYC** (420+ high schools as of 2024)
- **Adopted by LAUSD** and many other major districts
