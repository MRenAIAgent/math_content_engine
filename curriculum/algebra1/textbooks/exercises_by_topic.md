## 3. Slope and Linear Equations

### Finding Slope from Two Points
```
Find the slope between each pair of points:
1. (2, 5) and (4, 9)
2. (1, 3) and (5, 11)
3. (-2, 4) and (3, -1)
4. (0, 5) and (6, 2)
5. (-3, -2) and (4, 5)
6. (2, 7) and (2, -3)
7. (-4, 5) and (6, 5)
8. (1, -2) and (-3, 6)
```

### Slope-Intercept Form
```
Identify slope and y-intercept:
1. y = 3x + 5
2. y = -2x + 7
3. y = (1/2)x - 4
4. y = -x
5. y = 4

Convert to slope-intercept form:
6. 2x + y = 8
7. 3x - y = 6
8. 4x + 2y = 10
9. x - 3y = 9
10. 5x + 2y = 4
```

### Writing Equations
```
Write the equation given:
1. m = 2, b = 5
2. m = -3, b = 4
3. m = 1/2, passes through (0, -3)
4. m = -2, passes through (3, 1)
5. passes through (2, 5) and (4, 9)
6. passes through (-1, 4) and (3, -4)
7. parallel to y = 3x + 1, through (2, 5)
8. perpendicular to y = 2x - 3, through (4, 1)
```

### Answers
Slope: 1) 2  2) 2  3) -1  4) -1/2  5) 1  6) undefined  7) 0  8) -2
Slope-Int: 1) m=3, b=5  2) m=-2, b=7  3) m=1/2, b=-4  4) m=-1, b=0  5) m=0, b=4  6) y=-2x+8  7) y=3x-6  8) y=-2x+5  9) y=(1/3)x-3  10) y=(-5/2)x+2
Writing: 1) y=2x+5  2) y=-3x+4  3) y=(1/2)x-3  4) y=-2x+7  5) y=2x+1  6) y=-2x+2  7) y=3x-1  8) y=(-1/2)x+3

```

4. x = 2y and 3x - y = 10
5. y = 3x - 5 and 2x + y = 10

```

### Solve by Elimination
```
1. x + y = 10 and x - y = 4
2. 2x + y = 7 and x - y = 2
3. 3x + 2y = 12 and x - 2y = 4
4. 2x + 3y = 13 and 4x - 3y = 5
5. 5x + 2y = 3 and 3x + 2y = -1
6. 2x + 5y = 16 and 3x - 2y = 5
```

### Answers
Graphing: 1) (2, 4)  2) (3, 5)  3) (2, 6)  4) (3, 2)
Substitution: 1) (2, 5)  2) (0, 3)  3) (1, 5)  4) (4, 2)  5) (3, 4)  6) (1, 3)
Elimination: 1) (7, 3)  2) (3, 1)  3) (4, 0)  4) (3, 7/3)  5) (2, -7/2)  6) (3, 2)

## 8. Word Problems

### Number Problems
```
1. The sum of two numbers is 25. Their difference is 7. Find the numbers.
2. One number is 5 more than twice another. Their sum is 32. Find the numbers.
3. The product of two consecutive integers is 72. Find the integers.
4. The sum of a number and its square is 56. Find the number.
```

### Geometry Problems
```
5. A rectangle has length 3 cm more than its width. Perimeter is 26 cm. Find dimensions.
6. A rectangle has length twice its width. Area is 50 sq m. Find dimensions.
7. One leg of a right triangle is 5 cm less than the other. Hypotenuse is 13 cm. Find the legs.
8. The base of a triangle is 4 inches more than the height. Area is 30 sq in. Find base and height.
```

### Rate Problems
```
9. A car travels 180 miles in 3 hours. What is its speed?
10. How long does it take to travel 450 km at 75 km/h?
11. Two cars leave from the same point traveling opposite directions. One goes 50 mph, the other 60 mph. In how many hours will they be 330 miles apart?
```

### Answers
Numbers: 1) 16,9  2) 9,23  3) 8,9 or -9,-8  4) 7 or -8
Geometry: 5) w=5,l=8  6) w=5,l=10  7) 5,12  8) h=6,b=10
Rate: 9) 60mph  10) 6hr  11) 3hr
