# Algebra 1 Word Problems Collection

Real-world application problems organized by topic.

---

## 1. Linear Equation Word Problems

### Age Problems
```
1. Maria is 5 years older than her brother. In 3 years, the sum of their ages
   will be 35. How old is each now?

2. A father is 3 times as old as his son. In 12 years, he will be twice as old
   as his son. Find their current ages.

3. The sum of John's and Mary's ages is 40. Four years ago, John was twice as
   old as Mary. How old is each now?
```

### Money Problems
```
4. Sarah has $3.40 in dimes and quarters. She has 16 coins in total.
   How many of each coin does she have?

5. Adult tickets cost $8 and child tickets cost $5. A group of 12 people
   paid $81 total. How many adults and children were there?

6. A cashier has $725 in $5 bills and $20 bills. There are 50 bills total.
   How many of each type are there?
```

### Distance/Rate/Time Problems
```
7. Two cars leave the same point traveling in opposite directions. One travels
   at 55 mph and the other at 45 mph. In how many hours will they be 300 miles apart?

8. A plane flies 900 miles with the wind in 2 hours. The return trip against
   the wind takes 3 hours. Find the speed of the plane and the wind.

9. A boat travels 24 miles downstream in 2 hours. The return trip upstream
   takes 3 hours. Find the speed of the boat in still water.
```

### Mixture Problems
```
10. How many liters of a 20% acid solution must be mixed with a 60% acid
    solution to get 40 liters of a 35% acid solution?

11. A chemist has a 40% alcohol solution and a 70% alcohol solution. How much
    of each should be mixed to make 60 mL of a 50% solution?

12. Coffee worth $8 per pound is mixed with coffee worth $5 per pound to
    make 30 pounds of a blend worth $6 per pound. How much of each is used?
```

### Solutions
```
1. Brother: 12, Maria: 17
2. Son: 12, Father: 36
3. Mary: 16, John: 24
4. 12 dimes, 4 quarters
5. 7 adults, 5 children
6. 25 of each
7. 3 hours
8. Plane: 375 mph, Wind: 75 mph
9. 10 mph
10. 25 liters of 20%, 15 liters of 60%
11. 40 mL of 40%, 20 mL of 70%
12. 10 pounds of $8, 20 pounds of $5
```

## 2. Systems of Equations Word Problems

### Number Problems
```
1. The sum of two numbers is 52. Their difference is 18. Find the numbers.

2. One number is 4 less than 3 times another number. Their sum is 36.
   Find the numbers.

3. The sum of two numbers is 90. One number is 10 more than twice the other.
   Find the numbers.
```

### Geometry Problems
```
4. The perimeter of a rectangle is 56 cm. The length is 4 cm more than the width.
   Find the dimensions.

5. Two angles are supplementary. One angle is 30° less than twice the other.
   Find the angles.

6. The perimeter of a triangle is 45 inches. Side B is twice side A, and side C
   is 5 inches longer than side A. Find the length of each side.
```

### Work Problems
```
7. Working together, Amy and Ben can complete a job in 6 hours. Amy alone
   can do it in 10 hours. How long would Ben need working alone?

8. One pipe can fill a tank in 4 hours. Another can empty it in 6 hours.
   If both are open, how long to fill the tank?

9. Machine A can produce 100 parts in 4 hours. Machine B can produce 100 parts
   in 5 hours. How long for both machines together to produce 100 parts?
```

### Solutions
```
1. 35 and 17
2. 10 and 26
3. 26⅔ and 63⅓ (or 80/3 and 190/3)
4. Width: 12 cm, Length: 16 cm
5. 70° and 110°
6. A: 10 in, B: 20 in, C: 15 in
7. 15 hours
8. 12 hours
9. 2.22 hours (20/9 hours)
```

## 3. Quadratic Equation Word Problems

### Area Problems
```
1. The length of a rectangle is 5 cm more than its width. The area is 84 sq cm.
   Find the dimensions.

2. A square garden is enlarged by adding 3 feet to its length and 2 feet to
   its width. The new area is 72 sq ft. What was the original side length?

3. A rectangular pool is 20 ft by 30 ft. A uniform walkway is built around it.
   The total area (pool + walkway) is 1200 sq ft. How wide is the walkway?

4. The base of a triangle is 4 inches more than its height. The area is
   48 sq inches. Find the base and height.
```

### Number Problems
```
5. The product of two consecutive positive integers is 132. Find the integers.

6. The sum of a number and its square is 72. Find the number.

7. One positive number is 3 more than another. Their product is 88.
   Find the numbers.

8. The product of two consecutive even integers is 168. Find the integers.
```

### Projectile Motion
```
9. A ball is thrown upward from ground level with initial velocity 64 ft/s.
   Height: h = -16t² + 64t. When does it reach maximum height? What is that height?

10. A rocket is launched from the ground with initial velocity 96 ft/s.
    Height: h = -16t² + 96t. When does it hit the ground?

11. A ball is thrown upward from a 48-foot building with initial velocity 32 ft/s.
    Height: h = -16t² + 32t + 48. When does it hit the ground?

12. An object is dropped from a height of 144 feet. Height: h = 144 - 16t².
    When does it hit the ground?
```

### Pythagorean Theorem Problems
```
13. One leg of a right triangle is 7 cm shorter than the other leg.
    The hypotenuse is 13 cm. Find the lengths of the legs.

14. A ladder leans against a wall. The foot of the ladder is 5 feet from
    the wall. The ladder reaches 12 feet up the wall. How long is the ladder?

15. A rectangular garden is 24 feet by 7 feet. What is the length of the
    diagonal path across it?

16. Two cars leave from the same point. One travels north at 30 mph, the other
    east at 40 mph. After 2 hours, how far apart are they?
```

### Solutions
```
Area: 1. 7×12 cm  2. 6 ft  3. 5 ft  4. b=12, h=8 in
Numbers: 5. 11, 12  6. 8 or -9  7. 8, 11  8. 12, 14
Projectile: 9. t=2s, h=64ft  10. t=6s  11. t=3s  12. t=3s
Pythagorean: 13. 5, 12 cm  14. 13 ft  15. 25 ft  16. 100 miles
```

## 6. Function Word Problems

### Linear Functions
```
1. A taxi charges $3 plus $2 per mile. Write a function for the cost C based on
   miles m. What is the cost for a 7-mile trip?

2. A phone plan costs $30/month plus $0.10 per text. Write a function for monthly
   cost C based on texts t. How many texts can you send for $50?

3. Water is draining from a tank at 5 gallons per minute. Initially there are
   200 gallons. Write a function for gallons G after t minutes. When is the tank empty?

4. A plumber charges $50 for a house call plus $40 per hour. Write a function
   for cost C after h hours. If a job costs $210, how long did it take?
```

### Exponential Functions
```
5. A population of 1000 bacteria doubles every 3 hours. Write a function for
   population P after t hours (where t is a multiple of 3). What is the
   population after 12 hours?

6. A car worth $25,000 depreciates 15% each year. Write a function for value V
   after t years. What is it worth after 3 years?

7. $5000 is invested at 4% annual interest compounded yearly. Write a function
   for amount A after t years. What is the balance after 5 years?
```

### Solutions
```
Linear:
1. C = 3 + 2m; $17
2. C = 30 + 0.10t; 200 texts
3. G = 200 - 5t; t = 40 minutes
4. C = 50 + 40h; 4 hours

Exponential:
5. P = 1000 · 2^(t/3); 16,000 bacteria
6. V = 25000(0.85)^t; ≈$15,353
7. A = 5000(1.04)^t; ≈$6,083
```

## 7. Challenge Problems

```
1. In 5 years, a father will be twice as old as his son. The sum of their
   current ages is 55. How old is each now?

2. A store is having a sale: 20% off, then an additional 10% off the sale price.
   If an item's original price is $100, what is the final price? Is this the
   same as 30% off? Explain.

3. Two trains leave stations 400 miles apart, heading toward each other.
   One travels at 55 mph, the other at 65 mph. A bird flies between them at
   80 mph, starting when the trains start. How far does the bird fly before
   the trains meet?

4. A rectangular garden has a perimeter of 60 feet. If the length is decreased
   by 5 feet and the width is increased by 3 feet, the area increases by
   15 sq ft. Find the original dimensions.

5. Working together, Alice, Bob, and Carol can complete a job in 2 hours.
   Alice alone can do it in 4 hours, and Bob alone in 6 hours.
   How long would Carol need working alone?
```

### Solutions
```
1. Father: 35, Son: 20
2. $72; No, 30% off would be $70. The 10% discount applies to the already-reduced price.
3. The bird flies 266⅔ miles (the trains meet in 3⅓ hours, bird flies 80 mph)
4. Length: 18 ft, Width: 12 ft
5. 12 hours
```
